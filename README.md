## Key Features of Industrial PoE Media Converters

- Converts Ethernet copper (RJ45) to fiber optic links  
- Integrated PoE/PoE+ support for powered devices  
- Industrial-grade design for extreme temperatures and environments  
- Supports IEEE 802.3af / 802.3at PoE standards (model dependent)  
- Long-distance fiber transmission capabilities  
- DIN-rail and wall-mount installation options  
- LED indicators for power, PoE, link, and activity status

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is an industrial PoE media converter?  
An industrial PoE media converter converts Ethernet copper to fiber while supplying power to PoE-enabled devices, designed specifically to operate reliably in harsh industrial conditions.

### Do these converters support gigabit Ethernet?  
Yes — many models support 10/100/1000 Mbps speeds to accommodate high-bandwidth industrial and surveillance applications.

### Can I choose fiber type and transmission distance?  
Yes — models are available for both single-mode and multi-mode fiber, allowing customization of reach based on deployment requirements.
